## Introduction
Agri-Assist is an AI-powered agricultural marketplace and assistance platform. It connects farmers, agricultural experts, and buyers while providing smart tools like crop disease detection and tailored recommendations.

## User Roles
The platform supports three primary user roles:
- **Buyer/Standard User:** Can browse products, add items to the cart, make purchases, use AI tools, and participate in forums.
- **Seller (Farmer/Vendor):** Can list products, manage inventory, process orders, and communicate with buyers. 
- **Admin:** Manages platform health, oversees users/sellers, processes verification, and monitors platform statistics.

## Getting Started
1. **Registration:** Create an account by providing basic details. To become a seller, you may need to complete an extended profile (including citizenship verification).
2. **Login:** Use your credentials to log in and access your personalized dashboard.

## Key Features

### Authentication & Profile
- **Account Creation/Login:** Secure access to your account.
- **Profile Management:** Update personal details and notification preferences.

### Marketplace (Products & Cart)
- **Browse & Search:** Find agricultural products, filter by categories, and view detailed descriptions and testimonials.
- **Wishlist:** Save products you are interested in for later.
- **Cart & Checkout:** Add items to your cart and proceed to secure checkout (Khalti payment gateway integration supported).
- **Order Tracking:** Track your order status in real-time under the "My Orders" section.

### AI Features (Detection & Recommendation)
- **Crop Disease Detection:** Upload images of your crops to identify potential diseases and get actionable insights.
- **Crop/Fertilizer Recommendations:** Get tailored agricultural recommendations to optimize your farming yield.

### Community & Support (Forum & Chat)
- **Forum & Blogs:** Read articles, ask questions, and share knowledge with the community.
- **Direct Messaging:** Chat directly with sellers regarding product queries or negotiation.
- **Reminders & Tasks:** Set specific tasks and reminders using the integrated task module.

## Seller Guidelines
- **Dashboard Overview:** Monitor your active listings, pending orders, and sales statistics.
- **Product Management:** Add new agricultural products, upload images, update pricing, and set stock quantities.
- **Order Fulfillment:** Review incoming orders, update their status (e.g., Shipped, Delivered), and manage customer communications.
- **Deals:** Set up special deals or discounts on your products.

## Admin Dashboard
- **User Management:** Oversee all buyer and seller accounts, including handling verification processes (e.g., citizenship uploads).
- **Moderation:** Monitor forum posts, blogs, and testimonials to ensure compliance with platform guidelines.
- **Platform Analytics:** View total sales, revenue, active sessions, and system health statistics.
